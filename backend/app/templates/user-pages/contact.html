Facebook

By far the most popular way for club members to stay in touch electronically is though the club Facebook group.

Join it today!

The Facebook group is intended for club members: it is a great place to discuss your games, recruit players, and generally chat. All events are publicised though this group (though not necessarily exclusively).

The Unigames Mailing List

Unigames has a mailing list which is used by the committee to communicate important information to the members. It is recommended that all members join the list.

To join the mailing list you will first need an email account. Luckily these are now easy to come by:

  • No-frills Method
    Send an email to unigames@ucc.gu.uwa.edu.au with just the word "subscribe" (again, no brackets) in the subject or body. Well done.
  • WWW Method
    Visit our online subscription manager.
